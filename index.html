Poem for Gabča...

I'm not a poetist, but as a man I can say, you are too beautiful to let you go by. I can't hold it no more, I have to say, fell in love again when you texted me that day.

I heard that you got heartbroken, let me fix it for you. You can take my heart, It's waiting for you.

I didn't know how to tell you anyway, so I made a poem to tell you, come fuck me the hard way.

I want to ask you out as time slips by, But I don't know how should I. I needed to tell you so desperately, so I could ease my mind.

I love you so much, you don't even realize. I sent you pickup lines, to let you realize.
